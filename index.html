02Research

Research I've worked on.

A mix of lab and literature work in immunology and gene therapy, plus my own survey study on healthcare access.

01
2026 · Antioxidants (Basel) · PMID 42193171
Oxidative-Stress Molecular Signatures in Immune-Mediated Diseases
University of Miami Diabetes Research Institute · Research Trainee
A peer-reviewed systematic review that used machine learning and systems biology to study oxidative stress across multiple sclerosis, lupus, rheumatoid arthritis, Sjögren's syndrome, and IBD, pointing to genes useful for biomarkers and targeted therapy.
Mittal R, Valerio EA, Mutha V, Raj A, Hirani K

02
2025 · Journal of Drug Targeting · PMID 40539567
Gene Therapy for Beta-Cell Preservation in Type 1 Diabetes
University of Miami Diabetes Research Institute · Research Trainee
A peer-reviewed review of viral and non-viral gene-delivery approaches, including CRISPR, for protecting pancreatic beta cells and restoring immune balance in Type 1 diabetes.
Mittal R, Mutha V, Valerio EA, Hirani K, Arevalo G, Panchal A, Mittal M, Lemos JRN, Hirani K
↗
03
Florida Atlantic University · Original survey research
The Right to Thrive: Healthcare Accessibility for a Stronger Generation
Sole author · 198 participants · chi-square analysis, p<.01
A survey I designed and ran to study what keeps people from getting care. Transportation, affordability, and lack of insurance came up most often, and the barriers differed in meaningful ways by income and gender.
